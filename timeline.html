  • Registration(New User) August 1, 2020
    Get a digital signature from vendor and register as new user (type: natural person)
  • Provisional Specification August 1, 2020
    Form 2 to be filled. No deadline for this
  • Complete Specification August 1, 2021
    Form 2 to be filled. To be done within 12 months of filling Provisional Specification
  • Statement and Undertaking Jan 1, 2022
    Form 3 to be filled. Within 6 months of filling application
  • Declaration of Inventorship Feb 1, 2022
    Form 5 to be filled. With Complete Specification or within one month from date of filing Complete Specification
  • Request for Examination August 1, 2025
    Form 18 to be filled. To be done within 48 months from date of filing or priority, whichever is earlier
  • F.E.R. Issued Feb 1, 2025
    Time for replying to the First Examination Report. To be done within 6 months of date of issue of FER. Can be extended by 3 months. Total 9 months
  • Pre-Grant Opposition March 20, 2025
    Form 7A to be filled. After publication of application and anytime before the grant of patent
  • Grant of Patent August 20, 2025
    Patent will be granted (time depends on various factors)
  • Post-Grant Opposition August 20, 2026
    Form 7 to be filled. One year from the publication of grant of patent
  • Furnishing Information March 31, 2026
    Form 27 to be filled. Working Statements regarding the working of the patented inventions in a calendar year is to be submitted within 3 months from end of calendar year (ie 31st March). Eg: Submission of working report of year 2017 is due on March 31st 2018.
  • Expiry of Grant August 20, 2045
    Grant of patent expires after 20 years of grant of patent.
